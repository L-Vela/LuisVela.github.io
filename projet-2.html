Adventure Works Bike Shop

Description

This project features an interactive dashboard built using Power BI to support the leadership team of Adventure Works, a manufacturing company that produces bicycles and cycling accessories.

This project was designed in 4 steps:
- Connect and transform the raw data through a series of steps in Power Query.
- A robust model was built to ensure an efficient analysis of the data tables.
- A series of measures were applied with DAX formulas and calculated columns.
- A multi-level report was built according to the business needs of the management team. Graphic information contains high-level metrics such as orders, revenues and profits. The report is then structured to show data by region, analyze product trends and identify high-value customers.
