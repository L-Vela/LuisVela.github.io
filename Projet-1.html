Toy Store Chain

Description

In this project, I help a toy store chain with multiple locations to better understand their business performance.

To accomplish this, I have created a simple and interactive report that the management team can use to monitor key performance indicators and high-level trends. This dashboard is intended for the management team, regional directors and store managers. To meet the needs of each level of user, I created a table with three levels of indicators.

The executive overview contains high-level information:
- Total transactions
- Total revenue
- Total profit, sales targets and sales breakdowns.

The market insight provides regional managers with indicators by store location and by city. Finally, the product insight contains more granular data on products sold and their respective categories. This data shows revenue, profit, profit margin and average cost.
