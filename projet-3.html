Multi-national grocery chain

Description

In this project, I was mandated to exploit the information available from Maven Market, a multinational supermarket chain with sites in Canada, Mexico and the United States. The aim is to identify trends in transactions, sales, profits and product returns that will guide the company's strategic decisions.

The dashboard includes key indicators providing information in real time.
A table is provided showing the 30 strongest product brands performance in terms of annual transactions.
The geographical map and treemap bring an extra dimension to the distribution of annual transactions by city and country.
The treemap can be viewed at different levels, by country, state and city.
